首先你需要有一个[GitHub](https://github.com)账号，没有的话就注册一个

![register](https://images.cnblogs.com/cnblogs_com/blogs/610076/galleries/2034009/o_210920115135githubregister.jpg)

然后用电脑模式(或称为请求桌面网页或者用电脑)打开[我的仓库](https://github.com/vcgplus/vcgplus.github.io)

![teach](https://images.cnblogs.com/cnblogs_com/blogs/610076/galleries/2034009/o_210920152402Screenshot_20210920232244.jpg)

把用户名字换成你的名字，例如我的是VCGplus

![teach](https://images.cnblogs.com/cnblogs_com/blogs/610076/galleries/2034009/o_210920152419Screenshot_2021-09-20-23-23-28-346_mark.via.jpg)

建好仓库以后还要改一些东西，打开_config.yml

![teach](https://images.cnblogs.com/cnblogs_com/blogs/610076/galleries/2034009/o_210920154020Screenshot_20210920233023.jpg)

然后回到手机模式

![teach](https://images.cnblogs.com/cnblogs_com/blogs/610076/galleries/2034009/o_210920160944Screenshot_20210920235743.jpg)
![teach](https://images.cnblogs.com/cnblogs_com/blogs/610076/galleries/2034009/o_210920160958Screenshot_20210920235802.jpg)

然后根据同种绿色的字进行更改

![teach](https://images.cnblogs.com/cnblogs_com/blogs/610076/galleries/2034009/o_210920161012Screenshot_20210921000315.jpg)
![teach](https://images.cnblogs.com/cnblogs_com/blogs/610076/galleries/2034009/o_210920161022Screenshot_20210921000527.jpg)

然后是最重要也是最难的一步，你需要新建一个名为"blog-comments"的仓库，然后根据红框里的教程一步步完成。
(如果实在不会的话就评论说一下，看需求出我自己教程)。

![teach](https://images.cnblogs.com/cnblogs_com/blogs/610076/galleries/2034009/o_210920161055Screenshot_20210921000843.jpg)

改好之后往下滑，点击保存

![teach](https://images.cnblogs.com/cnblogs_com/blogs/610076/galleries/2034009/o_210920161114Screenshot_20210921000909.jpg)

这样，你的博客就搭建好了，会前端开发的可以自行美化加功能

基于[Jekyll](https://jekyllcn.com)
